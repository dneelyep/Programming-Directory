Welcome to Computers for Mechanics, a site focused on showing mechanically-inclined (or otherwise non-technical) people the joys of computers.

To get started, check out the recommended progression of subjects, the topic overview, or the alphabetical listing of subjects. Most of all, have fun! Basic Idea: Create a web site focused on providing articles, etc to teach mechanically-inclined people (ie mechanics/maintenance people/etc) how computers work. Ideas for Structure: An index of articles alphabetically. Search functionality built-in. A suggested progression of articles to read. Topics that list related articles. Extensive cross-linking. Eventually create a book with relevant info. Figure out licensing information.
